Events & News

Latest news and coming events at the museum:

Night at the Museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

The sleepover is available every first Saturday of the month. Contact us here if you want to join our night time adventures!

Energetica

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is at the Community Science Museum until the end of October 2022

Find more information about the temporary exhibition here.

Visiting Professor

It is our pleasure to announce that Professor Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’.

To attend the lectures, please get in touch with us here.
